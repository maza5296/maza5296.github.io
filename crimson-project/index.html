This article has been revised to reflect the following correction:

CORRECTION: June 2, 2014

An earlier version of a graph accompanying this article that compares by year the percentage of working graduating seniors going into consulting or finance after graduation was mislabeled. In fact, the graph compares the proportion of working seniors who will enter the two fields, rather than the proportion of all seniors doing so.
